Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

We are part of the community, owe our progress to people and society and depend on them for our existence. In recognition of this and in conformity with our CSR policies, we are active in various segments such as pollution control in our production units, care of environment within our vicinity, care of people who work for us and for their well-being.

Apart from this we have initiated various activities for upliftment of society.
