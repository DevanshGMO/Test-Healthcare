Exports Business

Abaris with its global foot prints have a significant presence in the African continent. We have made inroads into Europe & Russia and we have a definitive foothold in the South American Continent. Our manufacturing facilities have been inspected, audited & approved by number of countries including Uganda, Kenya, Congo, Nepal, Yemen, Nigeria, Ethiopia, Sierra Leone, Cameroon and many more in pipe line. Hence, our exports to above mentioned countries are at peak. Apart from this we are exporting directly as well through various other market operators to countries like Mauritania, Liberia, Myanmar, Mozambique, Mali, Botswana, Zambia, Ghana, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Angola, Rwanda, Ecuador, and Dominican Republic. Moreover, exports from these countries are under pipeline: Philippines, Russia, Tanzania, Zimbabwe, Sri Lanka.
