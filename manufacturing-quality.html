Success of Abaris Healthcare is founded on enlightened management, its team of hand-picked experts and last but not the least its manufacturing capability. Abaris Healthcare proudly boasts of owning one of the finest world class manufacturing facility in the pharmaceuticals segment in India. Vision of the management and eye to the future saw us make incremental investments in advanced equipment right from the start and then, as demand shot up, we easily scaled up production by adding machines that complement our existing lines.

Foresight led us to opt for automation and the highest levels of clean room technologies. Automation assures highest quality of products, virtually untouched by hand. Automation also includes strict quality controls at each stage, part of our credo of Total Quality Management.

Abaris Healthcare operates through two manufacturing facilities spread over 15 acres. One is in Rajpur, near Ahmedabad in Gujarat and the other is a more recent setup in Karjan, Himachal Pradesh. Both units are cGMP certified, well organized and structured for smooth process flow and ease of production management. Our units are segregated across various verticals: IV Fluids (Both Nipple Head & Euro Head), Liquid Injections (Both Ampoules & Vials), Oral Liquid formulations, and Tablets. Each section is handled by a team leader handling a cohesive team with full capabilities as well as responsibilities to assure productivity and quality conformity.

Key Manufacturing & Quality Highlights

  • WHO – GMP Compliance ensured at all time.

  • Aseptic Form-Fill-Seal technology obtained from Rommelag, global leaders in this technology. Ensuring No Human Touch in the manufacturing process.

  • Terminally Sterilized products by Super Heated Water Sterilizers from Getinge, Sweden.

  • Manufacturing under Class “A” area.

  • Delivering formulations in the form of Tablets, Capsules, Syrups, Gels, Injectables.

  • In-House Quality Control lab with top-of-the-line equipment, assuring quality adherence at all stages.

  • Double Pass RO system with EDI and Distillation Plant.

  • Prefabricated clean-room panels for manufacturing and filling processes.
